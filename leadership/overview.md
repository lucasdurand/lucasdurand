## Operating Style

I lead through **clarity, ownership, and leverage**.

I start by aligning stakeholders on the decisions and outcomes that matter, then design platforms and operating models that allow teams to move quickly within clear guardrails. I delegate ownership to empowered leaders and hold teams accountable for measurable outcomes.

I favor **platforms over bespoke solutions**, strong defaults over ad hoc choices, and automation over manual controls. Governance is embedded directly into workflows through templates, policies, and observability so that trust is built continuously rather than enforced reactively.

I measure success not just by delivery speed, but by durability: reduced operational friction, fewer recurring incidents, and teams that can move independently without sacrificing quality or compliance.
